# Fastest Path To Paid Work

## Positioning

Do not lead as generic freelancers. Lead as a three-person specialist engineering cell contracting through Coilworks GmbH and backed by InteSpring credibility:

**PCB electronics, embedded control systems, validation, and technical documentation for DACH mechatronic hardware teams, backed by motion-control, medical, robotics, industrial, wearable, UAV, and defense-adjacent proof points.**

That story is stronger than competing on commodity software marketplaces.

## Revenue Rules

- Break-even floor: EUR 100/hour.
- Normal target: EUR 120-140/hour.
- Specialist target: EUR 150-180/hour for PCB/control architecture, technical documentation for regulated or certification-sensitive development, RF/UAV control, safety-critical test automation, and power electronics packages.
- Minimum engagement: 4 paid hours.
- Standard analysis package: 40 hours.
- Prototype package baseline: 160 hours, adjusted by complexity, PCB stackup, component count, hardware risk, and documentation depth.
- Avoid low-rate marketplaces unless the project is strategically useful, fixed-scope, fast, and review-building.

## Priority Offers

1. **PCB Electronics And Embedded Control Review**
   - Buyer: DACH hardware SMEs, medical-device teams, robotics teams, industrial hardware teams, and engineering managers preparing prototypes or redesigns.
   - Deliverables: schematic/PCB review, control-interface review, BOM/component-risk check, DFM/DFT notes, test points, validation gaps, and prioritized fixes.
   - Rate: EUR 120-180/hour or fixed review package.

2. **Medical Device Documentation Sprint**
   - Buyer: medical-device startups, SMEs, engineering managers, quality/regulatory teams.
   - Deliverables: design history file support, architecture docs, requirements traceability, verification plan, risk-control documentation, engineer-supervised document cleanup.
   - Rate: EUR 140-180/hour or fixed sprint.

3. **System Architecture And Design Documentation Sprint**
   - Buyer: hardware/software product teams, R&D leads, grant-funded engineering teams.
   - Deliverables: architecture diagrams, interface specs, design rationale, test strategy, supplier-ready technical package.
   - Rate: EUR 120-160/hour.

4. **RF / UAV / Control Systems Troubleshooting**
   - Buyer: robotics teams, industrial test labs, embedded product companies, UAV teams, and defense-adjacent suppliers.
   - Deliverables: control loop analysis, flight/control integration support, telemetry/interfaces, RF power testing, MATLAB/Simulink validation.
   - Rate: EUR 120-180/hour.

5. **Power Electronics / Test Automation Support**
   - Buyer: electronics SMEs, medical/hearing-aid/testing companies, industrial hardware teams.
   - Deliverables: PCB design/review, Altium Designer and KiCad work, sensor and microcontroller electronics, wearable/medical hardware, product electronics integration, wiring/test bench design, LabVIEW/NI automation, HIL/HILS testing, EMC/EMI prep support.
   - Rate: EUR 110-150/hour.

6. **Human-Interactive Mechanical Systems Feasibility**
   - Buyer: medical-device, lifting-aid, wearable, exoskeleton, logistics, and defense-adjacent companies.
   - Deliverables: feasibility study, rapid prototype plan, kinematic analysis, supplier pathway.
   - Rate: EUR 130-180/hour.

7. **Prototype-To-Series Production Path**
   - Buyer: hardware startups, medical/industrial SMEs, engineering managers preparing pilots or small series.
   - Deliverables: EU-only manufacturing and sourcing path where needed, prototype sourcing, assembly coordination, quality-control loop, supplier-ready documentation, and broader lower-cost sourcing options when acceptable.
   - Rate: EUR 120-160/hour or fixed-scope coordination package.
